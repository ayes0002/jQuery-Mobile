Step 1 of 6

Everything starts with looks

To look like a Nicaraguan there are some basics standards that people there go by:

Shirts

The go to are polo shirts, dress shirts (Guayaberas or dinner shirts) and if you're young t-shirts. Usually not eccentric colours.

Close

Pants or shorts

Almost always pants. Doesnt matter if the temperature is over 40°C.

Close

Hair

If you're a man the go to is short and women usually have long hair.

Close

Accessories

Men wear watches and ocasionally a wristband. Women wear bracelets, earings, rings and sometimes wristbands.

Step 2 of 6

The way you talk

Because Nicaragua is a small country the chances of having mutual friends is very high. So conversation usually are very friendly, most men use the equivalent word to "bro" or "buddy" to refer to people they dont even know.

What you talk about

The go to topic to talk about is politic doesnt matter if its election season or not.

Any international sport event that theres a team representing Nicaragua becomes a hot topic to talk about.

Sports is also a very popular topic specially soccer.

Step 5 of 6

Where you go

If you are in Managua there are some sights (cathedral or the lake). About an hour from Managua there's Granada and Masaya which are smaller in population and infrastucture but very pretty because of their culture. There are two main beaches: San Juan del sur and pochomil which are great beaches and are about two hours from Managua.

The last step is how to behave like a Nicaraguan
